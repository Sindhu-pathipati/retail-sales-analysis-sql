
# Retail Sales Analysis Using SQL

Retail sales analysis project using **SQL**, **SQLite**, **DB Browser for SQLite**, and **Microsoft Excel** to generate meaningful business insights from retail transaction data.

---

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data using SQL to uncover meaningful business insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and regional trends. The analysis was performed using the Sample Superstore dataset and demonstrates how SQL can be used to answer real-world business questions.

---

## Objectives

- Calculate the overall sales revenue and profit.
- Identify the top-performing product categories and sub-categories.
- Analyze sales performance across different regions and states.
- Identify high-value customers and customer segments.
- Determine the most profitable and loss-making products.
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior and product demand.
- Generate business insights to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Dataset

- **Dataset:** Sample Superstore
- **Records:** 9,994
- **Columns:** 21

The dataset contains transactional information about customer orders, products, sales, profit, quantity, discounts, customer segments, and geographic locations.

## Business Questions Answered

This project answers the following business questions:

1. What is the total sales revenue generated by the company?
2. What is the total profit earned by the company?
3. Which product categories generate the highest sales?
4. Which sub-categories generate the highest sales?
5. Which regions generate the highest sales?
6. Who are the top 10 customers by sales?
7. Which products are generating losses for the company?
8. Which customer segment contributes the highest revenue?
9. Which states generate the highest sales?
10. Which products generate the highest profit?
11. Which states generate the highest profit?
12. What is the average sales amount for each customer segment?
13. Which products are sold in the highest quantities?

---

## Key Insights

- Technology generated the highest sales revenue among all product categories.
- Phones and Chairs were the highest-performing product sub-categories.
- The West region recorded the highest overall sales.
- California and New York were the top-performing states in both sales and profit.
- The Consumer segment generated the highest total revenue.
- The Home Office segment recorded the highest average sales per order.
- Office supply products were among the most frequently purchased items.
- The analysis identified both highly profitable products and products contributing to business losses.
